The Geriatricsconcern.com site is comprised of three primary user roles:

  • Geriatric Care Manager,
  • Client, and
  • Geriatric Family members.

The Geriatric Care Manager is the primary administrator of the Geriatric Client(s) and Geriatric Client Information. The Geriatric Client(s) represent the primary client with which the Geriatric Client Information is associated; typically a parent. The Geriatric Family are all other family members/close acquaintances associated with the Geriatric Client that can be provided edit or read-only capabilities to the details related to the Geriatric Client.

Geriatric Care Manager:

The Geriatric Care Manager is traditionally the user who is enrolling in the Geriatricsconcern.com site and has complete administrative capabilities, along with the Geriatric Client, to all data and aspects of the site. The Geriatric Manager and the Geriatric Client essentially can be the same individual, though it is recommended for the roles to be separate. At the time of initial enrollment and billing, the customer is required to populate the Geriatric Client and Geriatric Manager data points. This includes name and contact information for both parties along with some additional criteria related to the Geriatric Client. All other data points in Geriatricsconcern.com can be input by the Geriatric Manager (or Geriatric Client) at time of enrollment, or at any time subsequent to the registration process. A single Geriatric Manager can enroll and administer more than one Geriatric Client; e.g. mother and father. There is no limitation to the number of Geriatric Clients a Geriatric Manager can maintain from the site.
